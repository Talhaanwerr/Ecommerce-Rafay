General Questions

1. What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unworn and in their original packaging. Please keep your receipt for a smooth return process.

2. Do you offer free shipping?

Yes! We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. For orders below $50, a flat shipping fee of $5 will be applied.

3. How can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use that number to track your order on our website.

4. Do you have a size guide?

Yes, we provide a size guide on each product page to help you choose the right size. If you're still unsure, feel free to contact our customer service.

5. Can I change or cancel my order?

If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible. We can only make changes if the order has not yet been processed or shipped.

Product Questions

6. What brands do you carry?

We carry a variety of popular brands including Nike, Adidas, Puma, Converse, and many more. Check our products page for a full list of brands.

7. Are your shoes authentic?

Yes, all of our shoes are 100% authentic and sourced directly from the manufacturers. We guarantee the quality of every product we sell.

8. Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping fees and delivery times may vary based on location.
